The workshop

We will shortly be offering a workshop where participants can build their own PCBWriter for use in their own workshop or local Hackerspace/Fablab. It will take place at Dingfabrik in Cologne, Germany. The workshop will probably be partly funded by VDI and BMBF (awaiting confirmation). Depending on funding details, the cost for one PCBWriter will be around 100 - 200€. We anticipate that two or three participants will be building one device together.

We are currently planning to have the workshop on one or two weekends (Friday evening - Sunday). Given the amount of work still needed for development and organization, it will probably happen at some time in August 2013 or later.

Information on the workshop will be posted on this website as they become available. If you are potentially interested, please send an e-mail to pcbwriter@dingfabrik.de; we will notify you once we know more.

For members of Fablabs in Germany, travel and lodging costs may be covered by the "Verbund offener Werkstätten".
